About Me

Welcome to my space!

Leveraging over 8 years of experience in the semiconductor industry, I currently serve as a Full-stack Software Engineer(SWE). My expertise lies in developing and integrating cross-organizational projects using Python and SQL. As the Project Leader for Micron’s RMA Intelligent Abnormal Detector Tool, I successfully aligned multi-domain teams, including Micron IMC, Quality, and PE, to complete the tool’s first phase, providing AI solutions for detecting customer returns.

By defining the scope of Intelligent Manufacturing and introducing impactful tools, my applications now garner over 80 clicks daily, resulting in an annual saving equivalent to 20 headcounts. These tools add significant value by addressing critical tasks such as customer escalations, defect detection, and automation pipeline creation.

I have dedicated my nights to enhancing my coding skills, transitioning to the script development team.

My passion for coding is reflected in my top ranking on LeetCode. Additionally, I have earned 32 certifications from Coursera and other platforms, demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning.
